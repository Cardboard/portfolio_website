lower my standards

or lower the bar;

the blarney stone pub

is way way too far.

parking by rascals and

bypassing drugs;

surely i'm better

than all of these thugs.

the streets they are dirty,

the people they hold,

the unconscious mind,

and everything gold.

antimonuments and a

rubber band fight

are perhaps even awful

in the absence of light.

everything is,

is underappreciated,

tonka construction

for pc is dead.

labels and boxes

hinder and hurt,

the gentle soul's progress

and say it's the worst

like me for a week

after one brief stutter,

doubting myself and

the things i can offer.

one whole week gone

in glistening gutters,

dousing my fires

in the suburbs of mothers.
